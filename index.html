Technology

Built to Be Trusted

Hardware Security

Multi-layer protection: ESD guards, overvoltage protection, hardware fuse, and STM32 Firewall isolate critical firmware from the USB interface.

Data Integrity

Read-only filesystem prevents modification. One-time-programmable serial numbers and flash write protection make records legally defensible.

Precision Engineering

External 2.048V reference provides laboratory-grade ADC accuracy. This is the approach used in professional measurement instruments—not consumer shortcuts.

Power Intelligence

STOP2 deep sleep draws less than 1μA. Sensor power gating activates hardware only during measurements. Result: guaranteed 120-day operation on a coin cell.

Your Safety Net

Even If Nobody Presses START, Kaptar Has You Covered

From the moment Kaptar leaves the factory, Safe Mode begins logging temperature in a 4-month rolling buffer. If a shipment starts without manual activation, you still get the data. No other single-use logger on the market offers this level of protection.
